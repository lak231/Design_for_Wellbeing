Hello!

Thanks for using an attention tracker to improve your work habits!

This tool is best used for people who are looking to stay focused on a specific task on their own computer.

Instructions

When doing work on your computer, press the Start button to start the detector.

When a face is detected, we will monitor your attention and help you stay focused on your work.

Press the Stop button to end the session.

Reset will end the current session and start a new monitoring session.
